ABOUT US

We are a team of enthusiastic computer science students driven by a commitment to energy conservation and environmental sustainability. Our goal is to shed light on the growing issue of energy consumption in computing, empowering individuals and organizations to make informed, eco-friendly choices.

Our project, “Optimizing Energy Efficiency in Computing Devices” explores the impact of computing on energy use and offers practical strategies for minimizing this footprint. Join us on this journey toward a more sustainable digital world, where every small step in energy efficiency brings us closer to a healthier planet for generations to come.
